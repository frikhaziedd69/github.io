Project Type: Responsive Business Website for Manga Art Teacher

Goal: Create a clean, professional website to attract international clients, showcase teaching methodology, display portfolio, and capture inquiries.

Core Pages/Sections:

Hero Section - Immediate impact with clear value proposition

About/Teaching Philosophy - Her unique 1-on-1 approach

Services & Methodology - Step-by-step learning process

Portfolio Gallery - Student work progression

Contact Form - Clean inquiry capture

Design Requirements:

Clean, minimalist Japanese/manga-inspired aesthetic

Mobile-responsive (50%+ international clients will be on mobile)

Fast loading (optimize images)

Subtle manga/anime accents without being childish

Professional color scheme: dark blue/black + accent color (like crimson or indigo)

Technical Stack:

HTML5, CSS3 (Flexbox/Grid for layout)

Vanilla JavaScript for interactivity

No heavy frameworks for faster loading

Form handling (consider Formspree or similar for backend)

Specific Features Needed:

Sticky navigation for easy section access

Image gallery with lightbox for portfolio

Contact form with validation

Testimonial section (if she has quotes from students/parents)

FAQ section addressing international student concerns

Language toggle hint (potential future expansion)

Clear call-to-action buttons throughout

Content Structure:

Header/Nav:

Logo: "Manga Art" or her name

Menu: Home, Teaching Method, Portfolio, Services, Contact

Language indicator (flag icons optional)

Hero Section:

Headline: "Master Manga Art with Personalized 1-on-1 Guidance"

Subheadline: "3 Years Experience · Step-by-Step Learning · International Students Welcome"

Hero image: Her teaching or quality student work

CTA Button: "Start Your Manga Journey"

Teaching Methodology Section:

Emphasize her personalized attention

Visual timeline of student progression

Icons representing: Fundamentals → Character Design → Storytelling → Advanced Techniques

Portfolio Section:

Filterable categories: Beginner Progress, Intermediate Work, Advanced Pieces

Before/After student progression examples

Click to enlarge with descriptions

Services/Pricing:

Clear packages: Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced

International time zone considerations

Platform used (Zoom, Skype, etc.)

Trial lesson option

Contact Section:

Simple form: Name, Email, Country/Timezone, Current Skill Level, Message

Response time expectation

Alternative contact: email (business email recommended)

Footer:

Copyright

Social media links (if any)

Privacy policy link
